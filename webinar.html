The Center for Systems Integration and Sustainability offered a curated webinar series on Telecoupling: A New Frontier for Global Sustainability. The webinar series makes the telecoupling framework more accessible to scientific research on global sustainability challenges such as chronic hunger, loss of species habitat, water shortage, and the continuing rise of greenhouse gas emissions. The webinar series is made of four separate live online sessions divided in ~45 minutes presentation followed by ~15 minutes live Q&A session.
